I am a learner of Mathematics with a strong interest in the broad area of dynamical systems and differential equations. The broad goal of my research is to understand better the emergent behavior of networked dynamical systems, their invariance, and associated stability analysis, as well as the processes that control them. I use the theory from Algebra, Analysis, Differential, and Difference equations as my major tool. Currently, my research deals with emergent behaviors in networked dynamical systems with special attention to their invariance and stability analysis.
